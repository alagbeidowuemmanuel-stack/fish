Common Questions

How soon can you schedule an assessment?
We typically schedule assessments within 24-48 hours of your request. For urgent cases, we offer same-day assessments when available.
Is the assessment really free?
Yes, absolutely! Our initial assessment and consultation are completely free with no obligation. We'll visit your property, analyze your energy needs, and provide a detailed proposal at no cost.
What areas in Nigeria do you serve?
We serve clients across Nigeria, with major operations in Lagos, Abuja, Port Harcourt, and Ibadan. We have installation teams available throughout the country.
Do you offer financing options?
Yes, we offer flexible payment plans through our partner financial institutions. You can typically pay 50% upfront and spread the balance over 6-12 months.
