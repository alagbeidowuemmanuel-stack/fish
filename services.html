Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our solar services

How long does solar installation take?
Most residential installations take 3-5 days, while commercial projects may take 1-2 weeks depending on the system size and complexity.
What's the warranty on your systems?
We offer a 10-year performance warranty on all our solar systems, plus manufacturer warranties on individual components (25 years on panels, 5-10 years on batteries).
Do you offer financing options?
Yes! We offer flexible payment plans with reputable financial partners. You can pay 50% upfront and spread the balance over 6-12 months.
How much can I save with solar?
Most clients save 70-100% on their electricity bills and eliminate generator fuel costs. Typical ROI period is 3-5 years.
Do you provide maintenance services?
Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including regular check-ups, cleaning, and 24/7 emergency support.
